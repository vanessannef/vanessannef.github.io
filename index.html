About the project

I wanted to explore the ways that people responded to skincare products. I settled on this because I work for a skincare company and this data is incredibly useful as consumer insights is vital to the company. Finding out the ratings from the users was really interesting because price did not always correlate to best reviews which is something that most people would actually find interesting.

About the data

The data set was from Kaggle by Mary Lewis one year ago. The data was collected from reviews of skincare products from all types of companies and products, ranging from face mists, spf's, moisturizers etc. The data included reviews, price, product name, brand name, and number of reviews.

Takeaways

More expensive does not necessarily mean better (except when it comes to sheet masks and acne treatments). The highest rated skincare brand was The Body Shop. Consumers tend to write less reviews on brow products and the most on face mists.
